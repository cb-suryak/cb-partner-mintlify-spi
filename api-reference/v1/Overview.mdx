Our **Partner SPIs** are designed to enable seamless integration with Chargebee, empowering partners to extend and enhance our platform's capabilities. These SPIs provide a standardized interface for partners to connect their services, ensuring smooth workflows and exceptional user experiences.

## Key Features of Partner SPIs

1. **Tax Calculation**  
   Effortlessly integrate your tax computation services to ensure compliance and accuracy in real-time.

2. **Tax Registration Number Validation**  
   Validate tax registration numbers instantly to streamline customer onboarding and compliance processes.
